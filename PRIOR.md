Two obsessions dominate and reinforce: (1) how a capable LLM in an agentic loop should *retrieve* and manage its own evidence [doc_7858379e3e090aa7323601eec60c1175], and (2) whether AI can now run the research/post-training loop itself, where "the core bottleneck is research intuition" [hl_ffae6284cfaddcfe813bb7106a97dbf5].
